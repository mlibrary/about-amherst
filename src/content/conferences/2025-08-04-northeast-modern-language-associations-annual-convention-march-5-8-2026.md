ACP has a booth at NeMLA! Please stop by the exhibit hall to browse books and learn more about open access publishing.

We were visited by Mariana Past, translator of the forthcoming (May 5th release) book of poetry, *[Unbroken Nostalgia: Haitian Kreyòl Poetry in Cuba](https://www.fulcrum.org/concern/monographs/kh04dt031?locale=en)*, the first English translation and scholarly edition of the collection *Nostalji san pwen ni vigil: pwezi kreyòl nan peyi Kiba* (2016) by Hilario Batista Félix.
